Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

"Charlie and the Chocolate Factory" (English: Charlie and the Chocolate Factory) is a feature film by Tim Burton from the cycle of Christmas tales about spiritual values. Based on the book by Roald Dahl (the first adaptation took place in 1971 - the movie "Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory" with Gene Wilder in the lead role). A computer game was also created based on the book.
